What is Việt Pop Radio

   Việt Pop Radio is the only online Vietnamese radio station that is catered for young oversea Vietnamese and international audience. Our site provides background info about artists and a community for listeners to connect to each other. You can contribute to this station in many ways (some cost nothing).


Why Việt Pop Radio?

   There are a lot of site out there that provide user interactive music listening. However there isn't any Vietnamese radio that provide a more passive approach to music listening. For those who are at work, hanging out or just relaxing, you can always tune in to this station.
